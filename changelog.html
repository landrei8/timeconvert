v1.4 March 2026 Major

Preferences persistence, navigation arrows, timezone search, and a large batch of new cities added across all regions.

  • newSaved preferences — your city list is now automatically saved to localStorage and restored on every visit.
  • newNavigation arrows — « ‹ Now › » buttons above the timeline let users scroll left/right by 1h or 2h, and jump to current time.
  • newTimezone search — search by timezone abbreviation (EET, JST, IST), UTC offset (UTC+2, GMT-5), or IANA name (Asia/Tokyo).
  • new30-minute slots — timeline now shows 48 half-hour slots per day instead of 24 hourly slots for finer selection.
  • newNew cities added — Jordan (Amman), Israel (Tel Aviv, Jerusalem), Saudi Arabia (Jeddah, Mecca), China (Guangzhou, Chengdu, Wuhan, Shenzhen), Russia (Saint Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Yekaterinburg, Vladivostok), Mauritius (Port Louis), Romania (Galați), plus 12 new US cities and 15 African cities.
  • newCookies & Changelog pages — dedicated pages explaining data usage and tracking release history.
  • fixNow button centering — pressing "Now" now correctly centers the current time in the middle of the visible timeline.
  • improveBusiness hours highlight — green highlight now runs precisely from 9:00am to 6:00pm (excluding 6:30pm slot).
  • improveToast notification — small "Preferences saved" confirmation appears when city list is updated.
  • improveSite navigation — header now includes links between App, Changelog, and Cookies pages.
v1.0.0 February 2026 Initial release

First version of World Time · City Clock. Core timezone conversion with city search and draggable time range selector.

  • newCity search — search and add cities from a database of 70+ cities worldwide.
  • newTimezone conversion — all timelines correctly convert using UTC-based slot mapping, including half-hour offset zones like IST (UTC+5:30).
  • newDraggable range selector — click and drag across any row to select a time range; all cities show the equivalent window simultaneously.
  • newBusiness & night hours — green highlight for 9am–6pm, red for night hours (before 7am and after 10pm).
  • newDate navigation — browse across days using the date bar in the header.
  • newSynchronized scrolling — all city rows scroll together in sync.
  • newLive clock — current time updates in the city info panel when no range is selected.
  • newOffset labels — each city shows its hour difference relative to the first city in the list.
