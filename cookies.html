Short summary

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or collect any personal data. The only data stored on your device is your saved city list, kept locally in your browser using localStorage — it never leaves your device.

If you're looking for the quick answer: we don't track you, we don't sell data, and nothing is sent to any server when you use this app.

03

Cookies explained

A cookie is a small text file that a website can store in your browser. Cookies can be used for many purposes — some useful, some privacy-invasive:

  • Session cookies — keep you logged in while browsing. Deleted when you close the browser.
  • Persistent cookies — stay on your device for a set duration, used to remember preferences.
  • Tracking cookies — follow your behaviour across websites to build advertising profiles.
  • Analytics cookies — count visits and measure how people use a site.
World Time · City Clock sets zero cookies of any kind. You can verify this yourself by opening your browser's developer tools (F12) → Application → Cookies.
04

localStorage vs cookies

We use localStorage instead of cookies to save your preferences. Here's how they differ:

Feature localStorage Cookie
Sent to server Never — stays on your device Yes, with every request
Can track you No Yes, if used for tracking
Expiry Until you clear browser storage Set by website (days, years)
Storage limit ~5MB ~4KB
Accessible by Only this page, on this device Server + browser

Because localStorage is never transmitted to any server, your city list stays completely private on your own device.

To clear your saved preferences, you can either remove cities manually using the ✕ button in the app, or clear your browser's site data for this page:

  • Chrome / Edge — F12 → Application → Storage → Clear site data
  • Firefox — F12 → Storage → Local Storage → right-click → Delete All
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Third-party services

The only external service this page contacts is Google Fonts, used to load the DM Mono and Fraunces typefaces.

Service Purpose Data sent
Google Fonts external Load custom fonts (DM Mono, Fraunces) Your IP address and browser info are sent to Google's servers as part of the font request. Google's own privacy policy applies.

No analytics platforms, no advertising networks, no social media trackers, no CDN scripts beyond fonts are loaded by this application.

If you want to prevent the Google Fonts request entirely, you can use a browser extension that blocks font CDNs, or run the page offline after caching.

06

Your rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data of yours held by us to request, correct, or delete. Your only stored data is the city list in your own browser's localStorage — which you fully control and can delete at any time as described above.

  • Right to access — your data is already visible to you in the app.
  • Right to deletion — clear it yourself via browser dev tools, or remove all cities using the ✕ buttons.
  • Right to portability — not applicable; no data is held server-side.
